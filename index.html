Freeze time.

Real-time hand tracking in the browser. Open two hands to frame a rectangle, pinch to freeze it. In PATCH mode that region stamps onto the live feed — stack a dozen, each caught at its own instant. In PORTHOLE mode the whole frame freezes except that region, which stays live. Press M to switch modes, grab to move or resize what you froze, S to save, fists to clear. Nothing leaves your device.

Needs webcam access · works in Chrome on a laptop
